About us

Four TV Studios from public service televisions of each of the Visegrad Four countries prepare a common TV magazine monthly.

All the four partners have always been equal cooperators. We began to prepare a common television magazine called QUARTET in 2000. Since then we have never ceased to produce it, although the directors general and political situations changed. RTVS - TV Studio Košice, ČT - TV Studio Brno and Ostrava, MTVA - Duna TV Szeged, TVP - TVP3 Rzeszow.
